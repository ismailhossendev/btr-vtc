About BTR

On 2nd March 2019, Bangladeshi Truckers Revolution started their Journey with very few members. Many people helped to grow BTR fast and now BTR is one of the Largest VTC of TruckersMP and biggest VTC of Bangladesh. At present, BTR has 330+ Registered Members.
